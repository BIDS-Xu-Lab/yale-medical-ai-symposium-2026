Overview

About the Symposium

Hosted by Yale Center for Clinical Investigation (YCCI) and Yale Bioinformatics and Biostatistics Innovation Center (YBIC), the 2026 Yale Medical AI Symposium brings together researchers, clinicians, and technologists to explore the frontier of AI in medicine.

The event brings together investigators and clinicians from Yale, other CTSA institutions, AI companies, and industry to showcase cutting-edge work in clinical AI.

Who Should Attend

This symposium is open to all interested in AI in healthcare and medicine, including:

• CTSA institutions investigators and trainees
• Clinicians and clinical researchers
• Biomedical and health informaticists
• Health data scientists and engineers
• Medical AI product developers
• Health system administrators

Symposium Objectives

Innovative AI Methods

Develop and showcase innovative Large Language Model (LLM) and multimodal AI methods to address clinical challenges across CTSA institutions.

Cross-CTSA Collaboration

Promote collaboration and knowledge transfer among CTSA institutions to accelerate impactful AI-driven clinical trials.

Responsible & Reproducible AI

Address challenging issues related to responsible AI, including bias, fairness, reproducibility, and interpretability across all aspects.

Multi-site Infrastructure

Build infrastructure opportunities for multi-site AI studies and analyses to support broad and equitable clinical AI research.

Call for Posters (CFP)

We are excited to invite you to submit research, methods, platforms, and approaches for AI to transform clinical care, health equity, and biomedical research across multiple CTSA institutions. Poster presentations are welcome from any department.

Submit Abstract
Suggested Topic Areas
  • AI/ML in clinical care & population health
  • LLMs & multimodal AI for clinical use
  • Clinical NLP & EHR-based phenotyping
  • AI-enabled trials & real-world evidence
  • Data harmonization & AI-ready infrastructure
  • Responsible & trustworthy AI in healthcare
  • Evaluation & deployment in clinical settings
  • CTSA AI platforms & workforce development
Submission Guidelines
  • Open to CTSA-affiliated institutions and members
  • Poster format: A0 portrait (33.1" × 46.8") preferred
  • Presentation format: In-person only (300–500 word abstract, 1 optional figure/table)
  • Abstract length: 300 words maximum (excluding title and authors), with optional figures
  • Author for correspondence: first author or designated contact person for the submission
